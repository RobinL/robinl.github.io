---
title: "Distilling artifacts"
date: "2026-05-16"
tags: ["AI", "Work"]
---

It's now possible to generate huge numbers of artifacts at little cost. These artifacts can help us understand and solve business problems.

The human's job is increasingly to distill them down into the smallest possible form that's useful to the business.

At least for me, it feels like there's a massive explosion of information being produced: research, working code, prototypes, notes. I know I'm going in the right direction if it helps me understand the problems and solutions well enough that I can then throw almost all of it away.
