title: "Agents and office work"
date: "2026-05-25"
tags: ["AI", "Work"]
---

Increasingly convinced that in the next two years or so we'll see agents have a similar effect on all office work as they've had on software development. So AI will start to have a much more tangible effect on peoples' jobs outside of coding.

The sense I get is that, when hooked up to Gmail, Docs etc., the models "understand" enough to do a lot of work. They're just limited by computer use and image parsing being slow.
