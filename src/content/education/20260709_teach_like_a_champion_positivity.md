**Bad version:**

> Why are you all so lazy? Nobody's trying. Get on with your work.

**Positive framing version:**

> Pens moving. First line: write the equation. I can see Aisha and Ben have already started with 3/4 x ? = 15. Good. Everyone else, same first step now. If you're stuck, write 3 parts = 15, then find one part. You've got 30 seconds.
