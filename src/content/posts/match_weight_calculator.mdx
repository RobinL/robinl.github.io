### Conversion Formulas:

<div className="match-weight-formulas">

$$
\text{Partial Match Weight} = \omega
$$

$$
\text{Bayes Factor} = K
$$

$$
\text{Probability} = p
$$

$$
K = 2^{\omega}
$$

$$
p = \frac{K}{1 + K} = \frac{2^{\omega}}{1 + 2^{\omega}}
$$

$$
K = \frac{p}{1 - p}
$$

$$
\omega = \log_{2}(K) = \log_{2}\left(\frac{p}{1 - p}\right)
$$

</div>
